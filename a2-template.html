Dataset

Jobs data present in VAST 2022 Challenge specify various characteristics regarding an individual employer.
The main focus in this dataset is the education Requirement, days to work, and hourly rate corresponding to a particular employer. Additionally, other details are job id, job start time, and job end time.
In this dataset not only the employer is distinguished by the days to work but also by the fact of their education requirement for a job.

The data used in the assignment is available online : https://vast-challenge.github.io/2022/index.html

Initial Analysis Questions

  1. Overall how many days does an employer work corresponding to their education requirement and which is the lowest with highest hourly pay?
  2. What is the average hourly rate for all education types?
  3. According to days to work what is the time every employer starts and ends work?
